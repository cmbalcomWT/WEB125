Driving Permits
Due to the Beach Renourishment Project, Beach Access Permits are NOT AVAILABLE until the 2024/2025 season.

Four wheel drive vehicles are normally allowed with the purchase of a Beach Access Permit for fishing purposes only.

PURCHASING A PERMIT:
Monday - Friday from the Topsail Beach Police Department 8:00am - 3:00pm (812 S Anderson Blvd)
Saturday and Sunday at the Fire Department (816 S Anderson Blvd)
Please be patient if no one is readily available, as they are answering an emergency call.

COST:
Season Pass
$100 for non-residents
$25 for residents

Weekly Pass
$50

Required Documentation
Driver's License
Vehicle Registration
Fishing License (State-Issued)

The Topsail Beach permit decal must be placed on the windshield of the vehicle once purchased.

Wheelchairs
Topsail Beach has three beach wheelchairs available for those who have issues walking on sand. These chairs are first-come, first-serve, and are available through the Topsail Beach Police Department.

Rules:
-Wheelchairs are checked out daily, and must be returned by 9pm every day
-It is PROHIBITED to take the chairs outside the limits of the town
-The user is responsible for any damages to the chair while in their possession
-The Town of Topsail Beach is NOT liable for any injuries that might occur while the wheelchair is in the users' possession
